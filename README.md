# solfege

`solfege` is a reference-driven AI music creation app for people who want to turn mixed inspirations into original track ideas and, later, generated music.

Users bring 2-3 inputs such as:

- a hardcore or club track reference
- a meme clip or cultural reference
- a third "golden example" for mood, structure, or energy

The app analyzes those references, understands the creative direction, and helps produce:

- a structured music concept
- BPM and mood suggestions
- lyrics or hook ideas
- arrangement direction
- generation prompts
- later, short audio previews and longer tracks

## Core Idea

The product starts with:

## reference in -> creative brief out

Then it grows into:

- preview generation
- section generation
- longer-form music generation
- iterative editing and refinement

## Why This Matters

Most music AI tools feel generic.

`solfege` should feel:

- musically literate
- reference-aware
- creatively opinionated
- useful for both beginners and more advanced users

It should help users learn, not only click "generate".

## Product Direction

Version 1 focuses on:

- pasting 2-3 references
- setting BPM, duration, theme, and mood
- AI analysis of the references
- generating a creative brief and project direction
- saving projects

Later versions can add:

- short preview generation
- style tuning
- arrangement controls
- longer generation
- project iteration tools

## Design Principles

- Start with a focused workflow instead of trying to replace a full DAW.
- Make the first version useful without perfect long-form generation.
- Keep the product creative, sharp, and culturally expressive.
- Respect legal boundaries around copyrighted and sensitive source material.
